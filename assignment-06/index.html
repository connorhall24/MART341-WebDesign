Grandma Pita's Homemade Tortillas


Ingredients

  • 6 cups of flour
  • 3 tablespoons of baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 5 tablespoons of lard
  • Anywhere between 1/4 cup and 1/2 cup of lukewarm water, not too hot, not too cold

Directions

  1. Start mixing all ingredients except the water together in a large bowl with your hands.
  2. Gradually pour in water while mixing.
  3. Knead the dough until it forms a ball and cleans the bowl.
  4. Turn flat iron or griddle on to medium heat.
  5. Start breaking the dough apart into small, flat discs, about the size of your palm.
  6. Place your tortillas on your griddle, and flip when they bubble up.
  7. Repeat until out of dough, and you should have about a dozen fresh, delicious tortillas!
